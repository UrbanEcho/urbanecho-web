Beyond describing any city's transport ecosystem, UrbanEcho allows you to step directly into it. With multiple cities now available on the platform, practitioners can interact with rich, multi-layered urban environments and uncover insights at a depth typically reserved for custom modelling projects.

When you log in, you enter a spatially rich interface where different layers of the urban system, movement, accessibility, demographics, land use, infrastructure, can be explored seamlessly. Users can instantly understand how reachable key destinations are under different assumptions, compare connectivity patterns, and explore accessibility metrics at high spatial resolution. As you adjust views or parameters, the platform updates dynamically, creating an intuitive, exploratory modelling experience.

For mobility researchers and transport consultants, UrbanEcho enables deeper behavioural insights. Where available, the platform integrates activity-based simulation outputs, allowing users to examine travel demand, temporal variation, behavioural patterns, and emergent mode dynamics, all without needing to set up or run the simulations themselves. You can visually explore flows, trip purposes, and temporal profiles, or combine them with your own geospatial data to produce analysis tailored to your project.

One of UrbanEcho's most powerful capabilities is the ability to interact with transport and accessibility outputs using your own contextual layers. Users can upload zoning datasets, project proposals, POIs, demographic indicators, policy areas, or network changes, and UrbanEcho automatically aligns these with the existing model environment. This allows you as a consultant to validate assumptions, as a researcher to prototype hypotheses, and as a planner to evaluate the implications of future interventions in minutes.

For teams needing rapid synthesis, the UrbanEcho AI Coplanner Echo acts as a domain-aware planning assistant. As you create scenarios or explore changes, from infrastructure adjustments to policy shifts, the Echo assists you and generates structured summaries, comparative insights, scenario–baseline differences, and narrative interpretations. These outputs accelerate the production of internal memos, tender documentation, or stakeholder communications.

Put simply: UrbanEcho is here to provide urban intelligence. It is a working analytical environment that enables practitioners to test ideas, benchmark assumptions, simulate behaviour, and communicate evidence, quickly, intuitively, and at scale.
