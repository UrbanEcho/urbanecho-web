Bogotá is rapidly emerging as a Latin American hub for applied urban innovation. Through UrbanEcho's collaboration with Bogotá Científica and the recent [Urban AI Workshop](https://www.cybercolombia.org/events/urbanecho_2026/) co-hosted with CyberColombia and the Distrital University, foundational work for Bogotá's digital twin is already progressing.

### Structured scenario domains

- **E-mobility transition**
  Fleet electrification pathways and charging infrastructure
- **Flood resilience**
  Climate adaptation for vulnerable urban corridors
- **Urban security**
  Spatial safety pattern analysis and intervention design
- **Road safety**
  Behavioural modelling of crash risk under changes

> **WORKSHOP OUTCOME**
>
> Participants from planning, transport, security, water management, and data institutions reframed priority city challenges into simulation-ready research designs — with variables, datasets, and scenario structures explicitly defined.

### What the digital twin will enable

1. **Synthetic population**: Realistic virtual population via Bayesian network learning
2. **Scenario testing**: Structured domains with co-created KPI frameworks
3. **Agent-based simulation**: Large-scale behavioural simulations aligned with research questions
4. **Pipeline integrations**: Prototype workflows already being assembled

UrbanEcho is now inviting early collaborators in Bogotá — public agencies, research institutions, NGOs, and innovation labs — to co-define the first implementable scenarios and support the transition from workshop design to operational digital twin deployment.
